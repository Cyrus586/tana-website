Who We Are?

Mctana Safety & Hardware Equipment is an exceptional supplier in the field of personal protective equipment (PPE) based in Uganda, in the city Kampala.Our Company is devoted to providing fit-for-purpose personal protective equipment & clothing to satisfy our individual customers' requirements. The main focus of our activities is therefore on the people. We offer them outstanding products, solutions and services. In this spirit, our brand promise "Your safety first" stands for maximum security at work environment.

We endeavor to continuously expand our competences to improve our operational functions by investing in product portifolio and human capital. Our stylish and dependeable products are geared towards complete customer satisfaction and building beneficial long-term relationships with all our clients. We serve small, medium and large corporates, prive & public sectors clients in the safety & security, construction, engineering and mining industy etc.

Misson: To be the leading supplier of safety and hardware equipments in Uganda and beond.

Vison: To enable a safe and secure working environment.

What We Do

We supply a comprehensive range of safety equipment which includes resiratory equipment, hand protection, protective clothing, hearing protection, eye protection; head and face protection and footwear. We also provide technical and customer support whereby skilled professionals help your business with the implementation and management of your company needs. With a character for high quality protective products, pioneering technology and valuable assistance we pride ourselves on exceeding our clients' expectations.
